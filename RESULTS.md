## üß™ Validation Methodology

All sample outputs were generated using:
- **LLM Model**: Google Gemini 1.5 Pro
- **Embedding Model**: nomic-embed-text (1536-dim, zero-padded)
- **Vector Search**: pgvector with HNSW indices (m=16, ef_construction=64)
- **Reranking**: CrossEncoder ms-marco-MiniLM-L-6-v2
- **Scoring Weights**: 35% skills, 25% semantic, 25% experience, 15% education

Parsed JSON outputs follow [JSON Resume Schema](https://jsonresume.org/) specification.
